Course Focus Distribution

We emphasize learning by doing. The majority of your time will be spent on hands-on labs and building projects for your portfolio.

Module 1: The AI Professional's Toolkit (Weeks 1-4)

The first month is dedicated to building a rock-solid foundation. You will gain fluency in the core technologies that every AI and Data Science professional relies on daily. This ensures you are prepared for the more complex topics ahead.

Portfolio Project: A comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of a real-world business dataset, presented with compelling data visualizations and actionable insights.

Module 2: Building & Deploying Predictive Models (Weeks 5-8)

Go beyond theory and learn the complete lifecycle of a machine learning project. This module bridges the gap between building a model on your laptop and deploying it as a useful, live application on the web.

Portfolio Project: A fully deployed predictive web application, such as a house price predictor or a customer churn forecaster, complete with a user-friendly interface.

Module 3: AI for Unstructured Data (Weeks 9-11)

Tackle the data that powers modern AI: text and images. This module introduces you to the exciting fields of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Computer Vision, opening the door to creating applications like chatbots, sentiment analyzers, and image classifiers.

Portfolio Project: An intelligent application for text or image data, such as a tool to analyze product review sentiment or an object detector for a specific use-case.

Advanced Skill Radar

Module 4: Capstone & Career Accelerator (Weeks 12-15)

The final month is where everything comes together. You will build a comprehensive, end-to-end AI solution for your capstone project while aggressively preparing for the job market through mock interviews, resume workshops, and portfolio reviews.

Capstone Project: A major, self-directed AI solution that showcases your full range of skills to potential employers.
