OUR MISSION

Statement

Our mission is to propagate the authentic teaching of the Quran & Sunnah as understood by the Awliyah & the correctly guided Ulema of the Ahulus Sunnah Wal Jam'ah. This is done under the supervision of Ahmad Dabbagh who is a Master in the Science of Purification (Tazkiyah Nafs) and the teacher of Tareeqah Muhammadiyah. Ahmad Dabbagh is also the Principal of the UK Academy of Islamic Sciences and the Director of Wiselife Academy.
